About Meraki Photography

Based in the heart of Alappuzha, Kerala, Meraki Photography specializes in capturing weddings, events, modeling, portraits, lifestyle, and travel stories. Our passion lies in creating timeless images that reflect real emotions, natural beauty, and unforgettable memories. With creativity, artistry, and a personal touch, we turn fleeting moments into stories you can cherish forever.
